BIM IN CONSTRUCTION

Building information modeling (BIM) has become one great tool allowing reduced construction cost and speeding up construction projects. BIM has the potential to avoid mistakes if a genuine planning effort between team member is carefully reviewed and shared. BIM software can break down and provide a better understanding of the construction project, that can be implemented by contractors and subcontractors. When used appropriately, BIM can reduce construction time, costs, and claims.

  • Provides a way to work concurrently
  • Integrates actual construction pieces and parts in the modeling process
  • Determining how the phases of a project should be scheduled
  • Detect or avoid construction and design issues early in the game preventing change orders and unforeseen conditions
